Helaman 1-16

The spiritual recovery of a people hardened by war. I am sure that Helaman had a rough go at it, but Helaman was strengthened by Lord in his calling. Samuel the Lamanite preaches unto the wicked and as a direct result, many converts are baptized by Nephi, the son of Helaman.

Favorite Scripture in Helaman

Helaman 5:41- "And Aminadab said unto them: You must repent, and cry unto the voice, even until ye shall have faith in Christ, who was taught unto you by Alma, and Amulek, and Zeezrom; and when ye shall do this, the cloud of darkness shall be removed from overshadowing you."

Nephi and Lehi had a lot of troubles, and these words brought peace to many that they taught. They taught me the same principles of faith and repentance through this scripture as well.
