Moroni 1-10

Moroni is given another opportunity to give his testimony of the Living Savior, Jesus Christ. He does so and provides some of the most important doctrinal scripture we have today. His last testimony, which could be seen as his "second" last testimony, strikes the heart with a power not seen in everyday life.

Favorite Scripture in Moroni

Moroni 1:3- "3 And I, Moroni, will not deny the Christ; wherefore, I wander whithersoever I can for the safety of mine own life."

Moroni refuses to deny Christ, and so do I. Simple as that.
