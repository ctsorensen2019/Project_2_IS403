As we have studied the Book of Mormon this semester, I have learned much. I would like to focus on self-confidence and faith.

Book Summaries Overall Summary

Testimonies

Alma 30-63

The final testimonies of Alma the Younger given to his sons as well as the war chapters. Great examples of how grace works in adversity.

Read Alma Now

Helaman

The spiritual recovery of a people hardened by war. I am sure that Helaman had a rough go at it, but Helaman was strengthened by Lord in his calling. Samuel the Lamanite preaches unto the wicked and as a direct result, many converts are baptized by Nephi, the son of Helaman.

Read Helaman Now


3 Nephi

The wicked run prevalent until the justice of God claims them. Those willing to repent and come unto the Savior are visited by Christ. The pinnacle of the Book of Mormon is contained in these chapters.

Read 3 Nephi Now


4 Nephi

Hundreds of years of peace spiritually and politically are enjoyed by the Nephites after the coming of Christ to the Americas. They were humble, submissive, kind, and shared all among them.

Read 4 Nephi Now

Mormon

The downfall of the civilization of the Nephites by their wickedness through the Lamanites. Moroni witnesses the death of his father and the end of his people.

Read Mormon Now

Ether

The rise and fall of the civilization of the Jaredites. As prophesied by the brother of Jared, the Jaredites fall because of the power of wicked leaders. This other civilization that came to Americas perishes about the time that the Nephites arrive.

Read Ether Now

Moroni

Moroni is given another opportunity to give his testimony of the Living Savior, Jesus Christ. He does so and provides some of the most important doctrinal scripture we have today. His last testimony, which could be seen as his "second" last testimony, strikes the heart with a power not seen in everyday life.

Read Moroni Now
