Mormon 1-9

The downfall of the civilization of the Nephites by their wickedness through the Lamanites. Moroni witnesses the death of his father and the end of his people.

Favorite Scripture in Mormon

Mormon 9:6- "O then ye unbelieving, turn ye unto the Lord; cry mightily unto the Father in the name of Jesus, that perhaps ye may be found spotless, pure, fair, and white, having been cleansed by the blood of the Lamb, at that great and last day."

I don't think this scripture is speaking about white or fair in terms of skin color, but as a symbol of the purity of your soul. That is what I like about this scripture, that Christ can clean even the unbelieving if they come unto Him.
